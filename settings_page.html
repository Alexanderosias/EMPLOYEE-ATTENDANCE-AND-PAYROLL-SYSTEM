Tax & Deduction Settings

🩺 PhilHealth Contribution (2025)

Monthly Premium = Monthly Basic Salary × 5% (Minimum: ₱500, Maximum: ₱5,000). Usually split 50/50 between employer and employee.

Example: Salary ₱30,000 → ₱30,000 × 0.05 = ₱1,500 (₱750 each).
🏦 Pag-IBIG Contribution (2025)

Employee: 1% if salary ≤ ₱1,500, 2% otherwise. Employer: Always 2%.

Example: Salary ₱20,000 → Employee ₱400 (2%) + Employer ₱400 (2%) = Total ₱800.
🏥 SSS Contribution (2023 Table-Based)

Based on Monthly Salary Credit (MSC) table. Includes Regular SSS, EC, and WISP. Employer pays larger share.

Example: Salary ₱20,000 → MSC ₱20,000, ER ₱2,400, EE ₱900, Total ₱3,300. Update table values as needed.
